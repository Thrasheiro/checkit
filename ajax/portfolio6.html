Survey of UX Design

Spring 2016

I sketched out tons of possible UI's for the fuctionality of our Roommates app. First, I used an iPhone app called Prototype on Paper to make a functional prototype right on my phone. As a group, we used the sketches to build out the app on Adobe Illustrator with the same functionality. After doing some user testing on our app in InVision, I took the findings and reiterated in Sketch App, which I quickly realized works much better with InVision than Illustrator.
